    How to use it

    Allow microphone access, press Start, and the meter will show a live weighted estimate of sound level. For the most accurate reading, compare it to a known sound meter and adjust calibration in Settings.

    • Use the app over HTTPS or localhost so the browser can access the microphone.
    • Disable loud music or fans nearby while calibrating your baseline.
    • Pause keeps your current session. Reset clears the chart and statistics.

    Meter settings

    Browsers cannot know your microphone's exact SPL sensitivity, so calibration aligns the meter with your device.
